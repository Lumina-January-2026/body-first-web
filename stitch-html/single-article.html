Cost Savings

How to Get Wegovy for $25/Month with a Savings Card

Body First Team
March 15, 2026 4 min read

Navigating the cost of GLP-1 medications can be overwhelming. While Wegovy has proven to be a game-changer for weight management, its list price can be a significant barrier for many. However, with the right savings card strategy, you could reduce your monthly out-of-pocket cost to as little as $25.

Understanding the Savings Card

The manufacturer offers a savings card program designed to help commercially insured patients afford their medication. This isn't a loan or credit card; it's a direct discount applied at the pharmacy counter.

lightbulb

Key Eligibility Requirement

You must have commercial insurance coverage for the medication to be eligible for the maximum savings. Patients with government-funded insurance (like Medicare or Medicaid) are generally not eligible due to federal kickback laws.

Step-by-Step Guide to Activating Offer

  • 1

    Visit the official manufacturer website and locate the "Savings & Support" tab.

  • 2

    Fill out the enrollment form with your basic information and insurance details.

  • 3

    Download the digital card to your phone wallet or print a physical copy.

  • 4

    Present the card information (BIN, PCN, Group, and ID) to your pharmacist *before* they process your prescription.

What if my insurance denies coverage?

Even if your insurance denies the claim, the savings card may still provide a benefit, typically reducing the cash price by up to $500 per month. Always ask your pharmacist to run the card as a "secondary" payer to see the final price.
