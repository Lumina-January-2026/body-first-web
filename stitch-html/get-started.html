Frequently Asked Questions

How does the cost calculator work? expand_more
Our calculator uses real-time pricing data from major pharmacies and considers your insurance provider to give you an accurate estimate of your monthly medication costs.

Yes, your privacy is our priority. You can choose a username and avatar to interact with the community without revealing your personal identity.
Can I track multiple medications? expand_more
Absolutely. The Body First app allows you to track GLP-1 medications alongside other prescriptions, supplements, and vitamins.
